About Us

We are a dedicated team passionate about making your wedding day unforgettable. At Events Prakruthi, we specialize in planning weddings inspired by the beauty of nature. Whether you envision a serene forest ceremony, a vibrant garden reception, or an intimate lakeside gathering, we bring your dreams to life with our nature-themed wedding services. Our expert planners work closely with you to create a seamless blend of natural elegance and personal touches, ensuring that your special day is as unique as your love story. Celebrate your wedding surrounded by the wonders of nature, with an unforgettable event that leaves a lasting impression.
